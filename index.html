WHAT'S
THE PROJECT ABOUT?

We are the world’s first hybrid exhibition which lifts borders of human emotions. Realities UA is an international charity project which combines a string of online and offline events. Art of photography, metaverse technology and implementing the exact location of the photos — we use every means to welcome foreigners to our world. Inner world of all Ukrainians.

Fill your heart. Feel like Ukrainians

our mission...

Our mission is to be historically the first multi-local hybrid exhibition which enables teleportation between the realities of various nations. We combine cultural diplomacy and fundraising to acquaint the world with Ukrainians’ present and to contribute to their future.
